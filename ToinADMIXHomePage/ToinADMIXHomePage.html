"What is RCJ?"


"RCJ" is the abbreviation for "RoboCupJunior", and has its leagus Soccer and Rescue.

We have participated in the "Lightweight" league since 2022.

RCJ is held in many countries and all of the 14 to 19-year-old students can participate the competition.

Lightweight is one of the Soccer Leagues with a special electronic ball.

It has some rules for robots' sizes, weights, and their moves.

"What do we do?"

Making Robots

We try to make better robots to do what we want, following the regulations.

The regulation is about robots' sizes, weights, and the sensors they have.

Robots which don't satisfy the regulations cannot be used.

However, to win the game, we need some heavy and big parts such as kickers, dribblers, etc.

Programming

After making our robots, we write programs to let them play socceer in the rules.

At first, every robots mustn't hit the walls. If they do so, they have to be out from the game for 30 seconds.

Also, not to lose the direct of the opponents' goal, robots need to fix thier postures.

Then, they chase the ball.
